Overview

Restaurant owners are some of the most overwhelmed people when it comes to AI: enormous pressure, thin margins, and no time to figure out which tools matter. Deep Fried Bananas cuts through it with a community and a dashboard that map AI to the twelve pillars that actually run a restaurant.

Owners can take the full path to franchise-readiness, or pick a single, outcome-focused fix and start there.

What I did

The work, broken down.

What I built

  • A dashboard that scores a restaurant across twelve business pillars and shows where AI moves the needle first.
  • Two clear engagement paths: a full franchise-readiness package, or single outcome-focused fixes like a profit-driven menu redesign.
  • A community layer so owners learn from each other, not just from tools.
  • Designed and built the product end to end. Currently in beta.
Outcome

A real product taking its first users, built to prove the model: give an overwhelmed owner a sequenced roadmap instead of a pile of tools, and adoption actually happens.

  • Twelve-pillar AI roadmap framework.
  • Two pricing paths, from single fixes to franchise-readiness.
  • Live dashboard and landing page in beta.
