What we cover on the calls

9 weeks. 9 workflows you can steal.

Every call gets recorded and goes in the library. Join the waitlist now and come to the live calls while you wait. By the time the platform opens, you'll already know your way around.

wk 1
Mon · Jun 1 · 10am ET
logo refresh art

Logo refresh without losing the soul.

Take an old logo and bring it into 2026, making sure it still works on the awning, the menu, a coffee cup, a hat, the Instagram grid. The kind of refresh a brand agency charges $5K+ to do.

wk 2
Wed · Jun 3 · 2pm ET
menu redesign art

Menu redesign, live.

Redesign a menu together: the look AND the math. Cleaning up the design, killing items that don't sell, repricing the ones doing the heavy lifting. (By the way, consultants usually charge $500–1,500 per page for this, and usually skip the margin part.)

wk 3
Fri · Jun 5 · 12pm ET
brand mockup art

Apply your brand to actual stuff.

Take a logo and brand colors and mock them up on a coffee bag, takeout bag, t-shirt, coffee cup, mug. Live. So you can see your place before you spend a dollar on the stuff.

wk 4
Mon · Jun 8 · 10am ET
storefront art

Storefront, refreshed for $0–$500.

Take a tired-looking storefront (the sidewalk sign, the window, the awning, the first 10 feet a customer sees) and mock up what a small-budget refresh would actually look like. Without hiring a designer or three weekends of guessing.

wk 5
Wed · Jun 10 · 2pm ET
merch art

Order merch you'd actually wear.

Take last week's brand designs and order them for real. Print-on-demand for hats, mugs, totes, shirts. No minimums, no inventory, no upfront cost. Live, on a real site, in 30 minutes.

wk 6
Fri · Jun 12 · 12pm ET
reviews art

Get more Google reviews (it's #1).

Google reviews are the single biggest reason new customers find your café. Most owners have 12. They should have 200. Build the system (QR card, follow-up text, the exact wording) so loyal customers can support you for free in 30 seconds.

wk 7
Mon · Jun 15 · 10am ET
loyalty art

Loyalty card redesign.

Most punch cards look like they came from a 1998 print shop. We'll design one that actually matches your brand, and works as a QR code so customers can't lose it. The kind of small thing that turns regulars into evangelists.

wk 8
Wed · Jun 17 · 2pm ET
facebook art

Facebook page, fixed.

Most owners' Facebook pages haven't been touched since 2019. Redo the banner, refresh the bio, fix the hours, set up the auto-reply. The page customers actually find when they Google your café.

wk 9
Fri · Jun 19 · 12pm ET
instagram art

Instagram grid that doesn't look like everyone else's.

Plan a month of Instagram posts in one sitting, using AI for captions, scheduling them out, and making sure your grid looks like YOUR place. Not a stock-template smoothie account. The kind of work a content agency charges $1,500/month to do badly.
