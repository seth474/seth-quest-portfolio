Overview

Elua is an early-stage startup with a strong analytical product and the same problem most teams face right now: too many AI tools, no clear order to adopt them, and a manual process holding everything back. Their analysis was rigorous, but it lived in static documents that did not do the thinking justice.

The goal was twofold: make the team genuinely AI-native in how they operate day to day, and turn their dense, twenty-page analyses into interactive experiences a client would actually want to read.

What I did

The work, broken down.

AI-native transformation

  • Migrated the team from ad-hoc ChatGPT use to a structured Claude workspace, including a custom Ask Elua assistant trained on their context.
  • Selected and implemented the right meeting, communications, and e-signature stack, in the order that made it stick.
  • Consolidated 30+ scattered documents from Notion into an organized Google Drive system.
  • Built a React operations dashboard to run the business day to day.

Product & UX

  • Turned their Depra 20-method analysis approach into a usable, repeatable system.
  • Designed a blue-and-amber visual design system for the product.
  • Designed 20 interactive, deeply-linked report artifacts with three-layer citation drill-down, so every claim traces back to its source.
  • Built the Elua Wins app and the Solan scoring interfaces.
Outcome

AI moved from a novelty to the operating layer of the business. Analysis went from static decks to interactive, source-linked experiences, and the team was left able to run all of it without me.
